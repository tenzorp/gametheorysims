Instructions

In this game you will be matched with one other student in the room. One of you will be randomly assigned to be Player 1, while the other person will be Player 2.

Stage 1: Player 1 will move first, choosing between “In” or “Out.” If Player 1 chooses “Out,” then the game ends, with Player 1 earning 100 and Player 2 earning 600. If Player 2 chooses “In,” then Player 2 learns Player 1’s choice and chooses between “Up” (Player 1 earns 500 and Player 2 earns 100) or “Down” (Player 1 earns −1000 and Player 2 earns −1000).
