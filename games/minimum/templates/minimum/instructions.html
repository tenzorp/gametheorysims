Instructions

 This experiment consists of 10 periods. In each period, you will be randomly matched with 4 other participants in the room. Your earnings depend on your choices as well as the choices of the people you are matched with. Each person will choose an effort level between 1 and 5. Your earnings depend on the minimum effort level chosen by you and the people you are matched with, minus your own effort. Specifically, your payoff is determined by the equation:

 Payoff = 5 * Minimum Effort - Your Effort

 The following table lists the payoff you will receive for each effort level that you might choose and the minimum effort provided by the entire team (including you).

 For example, if you choose 3 and the other four group members choose 5, 3, 3 and 2 then the group minimum is 2 and your payoff is (5 * 2) − 3 = 7. If instead, you chose 2 like one of the other group members, your payoff would be (5 * 2) − 2 = 8.
