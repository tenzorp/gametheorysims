Instructions

 In this study, you will be anonymously matched with another player. You will interact only via the computer and will not know which other student you are matched with. Each player will be randomly assigned a role: Row or Column. You will each simultaneously choose between four possible playing cards: Joker, Ace, Two, or Three. One player will then win 5 points while the other loses 5 points. Who wins and who loses depends on the combination of cards chosen. You will learn of the other player's decisions after both of you have made your choices.

 The Row Player wins if both players choose Joker or if neither player chooses Joker and the cards mismatch.
 The Column Player wins if exactly one player chooses the Joker or if neither player chooses Joker and the cards match.
